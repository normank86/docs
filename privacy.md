# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

RefTracker (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the RefTracker app and related services.

---

## Information We Collect

RefTracker does **not** require you to create an account and does **not** collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

The app may store the following **locally on your device**:
- Match details (games, goals, cards, notes)
- App preferences and settings

This data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

---

## Analytics and Diagnostics

RefTracker may use Apple-provided diagnostics and crash reporting tools to improve app stability and performance. These reports are **anonymous** and do not identify you personally.

---

## Data Sharing

RefTracker does **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data is only shared if:
- You explicitly choose to export or share it
- Required by law

---

## Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the data stored within the app. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## Children’s Privacy

RefTracker is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
